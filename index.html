FA AntiVira

FA AntiVira is a simple Anti Virus made by fabischau

Important: FA AntiVira has no real-time protection, so you should use it with another anti-virus.

Available for Windows 10 and 11

  • Simple Quarantene

  • Quarantine Signature Based Detection
